Weatheruous by Softklass - Privacy Policy



CHILDREN

Weatheruous is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We advise children under the age of 13 to not use this application.



DATA

We do not collect any data. The only data involved in this app is what you search for, and that is saved in the database on the device and later retrieved to search for weather based on those locations. No data of any kind is collected or shared.

LOCATION DATA

Unlike many weather applications, this application does not use location services to get the weather. Rather, the user can use the location search feature for locations to use.
